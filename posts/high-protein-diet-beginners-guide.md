Protein is one of the most important nutrients for sustainable fat loss. Not because it magically burns fat, but because it supports muscle maintenance, appetite control and daily energy levels.

This beginner-friendly guide explains how protein helps with fat loss and how to structure your meals realistically.

---

## Why protein matters for fat loss

### 1. Helps maintain muscle mass

### 2. Increases satiety

### 4. Supports stable energy levels  

Protein evenly distributes energy throughout the day.

- **0.7 to 1 gram per pound of goal body weight**
