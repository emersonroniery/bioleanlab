## What is metabolic adaptation?

Metabolic adaptation refers to the body's natural response to prolonged calorie deficits. Studies show that when we diet, our bodies often:

- reduce non-exercise movement  
- lower resting metabolic rate slightly  
- become more efficient at storing energy  

This adaptation is **normal**, and it's not a sign that your metabolism is damaged.

---

## How much does metabolism slow down?

Research suggests that metabolic slowdown is real, but the effect is often **smaller than people expect**.

A 2016 study published in *Obesity* followed participants from The Biggest Loser competition and showed that large, fast weight loss can cause noticeable metabolic adaptation. But in normal, gradual weight loss, the effect is modest.

## What does NOT cause metabolic slowdown?

Popular myths include:

- eating too few calories  
- skipping breakfast  
- slowing metabolism after age 30  
- drinking cold water  
- using "detox" products  

None of these magically "break" metabolism.
